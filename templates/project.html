Safe Kerala ‚Äì Migrated Workers Portal addresses the challenge of finding authorized workers from outside Kerala. This interactive, web-enabled application allows for seamless identity verification and worker registration. Police officers play a crucial role by adding verified worker details to the site, ensuring that only authorized personnel are listed. The portal allows users to search for workers based on their technical skills, providing a precise match for their needs. If any worker is found to have a criminal record, the police can block their profile from the site. Additionally, users can lodge complaints about workers directly through the portal, ensuring quick police intervention. The admin has the capability to oversee all site activities, ensuring smooth operations and facilitating the availability of authorized workers for users. The system streamlines the process of finding skilled labor while ensuring safety and security for all parties involved.
